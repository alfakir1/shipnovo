# ShipNovo Product Vision (Octopus Architecture)

ShipNovo is a 4PL (Fourth-Party Logistics) platform designed to orchestrate the entire supply chain through a unified digital command center.

## Core Philosophical Pillars:
1. **Orchestration First**: We don't just ship; we coordinate carriers, warehouses, and customs brokers.
2. **End-to-End Visibility**: Real-time tracking from origin to final destination across multiple modes.
3. **Seamless Integration**: A modular "Octopus" architecture that scales with API, EDI, and IoT integrations.
4. **Data-Driven Intelligence**: Using AI for route optimization and predictive ETAs.

## MVP Goal:
Create a high-impact, demonstration-ready prototype that showcases the core flow of a 4PL operation: Shippers booking, Ops orchestrating, and Partners executing. The platform operates in Yemen, focusing on shipments arriving to Yemen.
