# MVP Pages & User Flows

## Customer Pages
- `/customer/dashboard`: Overview of active shipments, tickets, and spending.
- `/customer/shipments/new`: Multi-step form to request a new shipment.
- `/customer/shipments/[id]`: Detail view with tracking timeline and documents.
- `/customer/tickets`: Support request management.

## Ops Pages
- `/ops/dashboard`: Global operations overview and pending assignments.
- `/ops/shipments`: Master list of all shipments with filters.
- `/ops/shipments/[id]`: Command center for assigning partners and updating status.
- `/ops/partners`: Directory of active logistics partners.

## Partner Pages
- `/partner/dashboard`: Shipments assigned to the current partner.
- `/partner/shipments/[id]/update`: Quick interface to update status and upload docs.
